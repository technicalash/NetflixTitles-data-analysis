# Netflix Data Analysis 

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Netflix's content dataset to uncover trends in content production, genres, countries, and audience targeting.

## Objectives

* Analyze distribution of Movies vs TV Shows
* Identify top content-producing countries
* Study growth of Netflix content over time
* Explore popular genres
* Understand audience targeting using ratings
* Analyze duration of movies and TV shows

## Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn

## Key Insights

* Netflix has significantly more movies than TV shows
* The United States and India are the top content producers
* Content growth increased rapidly after 2018, peaking around 2020
* Drama and Comedy are the most popular genres
* Most content is targeted toward adult audiences
* Movies are typically 90–120 minutes long
* Most TV shows have 1–2 seasons

## Dataset

The dataset used in this project is publicly available on Kaggle:

Netflix Movies and TV Shows Dataset
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shivamb/netflix-shows

## Conclusion

The analysis highlights Netflix’s focus on movies, dominance of specific countries in content production, and a growing trend toward TV shows in recent years.

## Limitations
The dataset is not up-to-date and may not reflect the latest content available on Netflix.
Some columns contain missing or incomplete data.
The dataset has limited features and does not include user-level data such as watch history or ratings.
